Link your AI projects into a brand What is this panel? AI Visibility Summary combines your AI Overview, AI Mode and LLM Monitoring metrics into a single score per brand, so you can see at a glance whether your AI visibility is improving and how you compare to competitors. How to set it up Link the projects that belong to the same brand — confirm a suggestion below or use the manual form. You don't need all three modules: link only what you monitor. How the score works The score is a weighted average of the linked channels only. A channel you don't monitor never lowers your score, and you can add it later at any time.
